How to get these printed

  • Support a local print shop (Recommended).
    Download the PDF version. Put it on a thumb drive or email it to a local independent print shop. This builds relationships in your community. Ask for "vinyl die-cut stickers."
  • Order online.
    Download the PNG version. Upload it to a service like StickerApp. They handle the cutting and shipping.
  • Print at home.
    If you have a color printer, buy "Avery 8.5x11 Full Sheet Label Paper." Use the PDF, but ensure your printer settings are set to "Scale: 100%" (Do not select "Fit to Page"). You will likely fit 2-4 stickers per page if you arrange them in a document editor first.
